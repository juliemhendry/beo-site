Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 12, 2025

Overview

beò ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you sign up for our beta waitlist or use our services.

Information We Collect

Beta Waitlist: When you join our waitlist, we collect your email address and optionally your name and feedback about what excites you about beò. This is processed through Formspree and stored securely.

App Usage (When Launched): The beò app will collect data about your phone usage patterns, mood check-ins, and intervention interactions. This data is used solely to provide you with personalized insights and improve your digital wellbeing experience.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Send you beta launch notifications and updates
  • Provide personalized digital wellbeing insights
  • Improve our app features and interventions
  • Conduct research on digital wellbeing (aggregated and anonymized)

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share anonymized, aggregated data for research purposes. Your individual data is never shared with third parties except:

  • Service providers who help us operate the app (e.g., Formspree for email collection)
  • When required by law

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time

Data Retention

Waitlist data is retained until you opt out or until the beta program concludes. App usage data is retained as long as you maintain an active account, plus 90 days after account closure for backup purposes.

Children's Privacy

beò is not intended for users under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 16.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email (if you're on our waitlist) or through prominent notice in the app.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or want to exercise your rights, contact us at:
